Current Inventions

Adobe Lightroom

Software

Adobe Lightroom is an app that can be accessed from any device. It is best known for its excellent features of image management by being able to find images quickly in a library-like catalog.

Adobe Photoshop

Software

Adobe Photoshop is mainly used to allow editors to crop photos and adjust composition. It also does a great job at correcting the lighting of photos.

ON1 Photo RAW

Software

ON1 Photo RAW provides you powerful AI assisting with all the newest editing tools and technologies built into the editor as well as other things like mask images, swap out skies, reduce image noises, etc.

Camera

Hardware

A camera is a device that allows you to capture and record pictures and videos. It consists of several parts that allows it to work at it's best form such as the lens, image sensor, shutter, viewfinder, and etc.

Camera Lens

Hardware

A camera lens is an important part of a camera that focuses light onto the camera's image sensor, making captured photos and images clear. It plays a important role in the quality, perspective, and overall look of the images produced.

Image Sensor

Hardware

An image sensor is a important part of cameras that captures and converts light into an electrical signal which results in a digital image. It is essentially a specialized electronic chip designed to sense and record the intensity and color of light falling on it.
